## Business Problem

Metro Sales recorded a **53% drop in revenue** compared to the previous period. The analysis aimed to answer three questions:

1. Which regions, products, and customer segments are underperforming?
2. Are there seasonal patterns driving the decline?
3. What specific actions can reverse the trend?

## Key Insights

- **Revenue dropped 53%** — the decline was concentrated in specific regions and product categories, not spread evenly across the business
- **Certain regions consistently underperformed**, pulling down overall numbers while high-performing regions held steady
- **A small number of product categories drove most of the revenue loss** — the problem is concentrated, not broad
- **Clear seasonal demand patterns** were identified, pointing to inventory and planning gaps during peak periods

---

## Recommendations

| Priority | Action | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| 🔴 High | Put underperforming regions on a targeted recovery plan | Region-level gaps compound over time if left unaddressed |
| 🔴 High | Review and restructure failing product categories | A small number of categories account for most of the revenue loss |
| 🟡 Medium | Align inventory planning with seasonal demand peaks | Missing peak windows directly costs revenue |
| 🟢 Ongoing | Track KPIs through the dashboard month-on-month | Prevents the same patterns from going undetected again |
